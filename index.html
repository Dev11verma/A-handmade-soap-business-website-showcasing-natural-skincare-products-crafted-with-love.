Our Story

The idea of creating homemade soaps started during the challenging times of the COVID-19 pandemic. With a passion for crafting and a desire to make something meaningful, we began experimenting with natural ingredients to create soaps that were gentle on the skin and the environment.

What started as a small kitchen experiment turned into a growing business! By 2020-21, we had sold over 1,000 units, supported by a wonderful community of loyal customers.

Today, we proudly craft soaps that blend care, sustainability, and love in every bar. Thank you for being part of our story.
